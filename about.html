Mount Scrapes Real Estate Ltd was founded in the year 2014 by Mr. Rubayat Bonim, a first generation entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in the construction of bridge, culvert, road and real estate industry, Mr.Kabir brings on board his unique vision, management practices and global approach to the function, expansion, diversification and management of the organization. As an integral part of the key management, he manages a portfolio spanning across residential, commercial and iconic landmarks in Dhaka city. With passion for business, Mr.Kabir is involved in the formulation of corporate strategy and acquisition, while providing guidance on execution and diversification plans of the Company.

Under his Leadership, Mount Scrapes Real Estate Ltd today manages a development portfolio catering to the ultra-Luxurious and Premium segment of over 5 00,000sq.ft spread across over 7 projects and pioneered in brand tie-ups with international brands like Machey. With a natural flair in realty, combined with an astute sense of Finance and Management, his foresight has enabled the ‘Mount Scrapes Real Estate Brand’ to create a differential for itself in the industry.

His vision is depicted in being the foremost to anticipate an opportunity in the premium residential space of 300 bighas of land at Dakhin Khan, Uttara at Dhaka’s most promising residential-cum-commercial hub –SPRING TOWN.

Mr. Bonim holds the position of Chairman of Mount Scrapes Real Estate Ltd.

Company Profile:

Mount Scrapes Real Estate Ltd is a full-service developers company specializing in residential building construction. The company brings many years of combined building experience and insures successful projects by working closely with their clients and a carefully chosen group of architects, subcontractors and suppliers. The company also has a complete cabinet shop that enables to create its own cabinetry and millwork for projects maintaining quality while keeping costs in line.

The experience of the company enables them to guide clients through each phase of the building process and they welcome and encourage client participation at every step along the way.

Establishing a budget, putting together a construction schedule, understanding the permit process and working through the many design decisions to be made, are just a few of the key aspects of their collaboration with their clients.
