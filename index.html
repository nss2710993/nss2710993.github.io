Wellcome to Mount Scrapers Ltd.

Mount Scrapers Ltd. a sister concern of Wits Group, was established back in 1992 as an indenting firm and over the years has grown into internationally renowned indenting firm specialized in Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients, Intermediates, Pharmaceutical Machinery and Pharmaceutical Packaging Materials.

Mount Scrapers specializes in sourcing Global Manufacturers with all regulatory approval like USDMF, EDMF & CEP for regulatory markets and also undertakes registration of new manufacturers and renewal of existing registration with DGDA.

Besides, Mount Scrapers being well connected with all the local Pharmaceutical Formulations Companies is well placed to offer diverse range of Formulations for both regulated and non-regulated market. So please contact us for any of your import requirement of Formulations from Bangladesh. We shall offer you quality products at most competitive price.

With young dedicated and hard working team Mount Scrapers look forward to achieve greater heights and entering new areas and serve the Pharma Industry at large.
