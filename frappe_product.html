FRAPPE PRODUCT

Metro Properties has opened premiere brand frozen yogurt and desserts at its own SAM TOWER building in the name MENCHIE’S.

Menchie’s is the premiere brand for frozen yogurt and desserts across the globe. Menchie's opened its first store in Valley Village, a suburban community of Los Angeles, California, on May 15, 2007. In 2010, Menchie's was awarded as the number one fastest growing food franchise in the United States and holds today the prestigious status of the largest self-serve frozen yogurt franchise in the world.

Mount Scrapes Real Estate Ltd is proud to launch this iconic brand in Bangladesh to spread joy to its community. Menchie's franchise community boasts talented franchisees of high moral fiber that are actively involved in their communities, and embody our core values. A "secret ingredient" to the Menchie's winning formula is our passionate, global, and growing family of franchisees who provide a magical place within every community. Every Menchie's store is a place for family and friends to come together and create lasting memories.

Every aspect of Menchie's will be standardized and tightly controlled. The menu at every store will consist of various yogurt flavors, dry goods, and fresh fruits that are partly adapted to the local market. There will also be a limited number of other items, and branded merchandise that will enhance the guest experience and develop the love for the brand in every guest.
